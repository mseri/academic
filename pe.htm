Activities

Spectre, Hats and Mats

This activity has been developed in collaboration with ScienceLinX and Leen van Wijngaarden for the Zpannend Zernike festival on October 6 2024.

With 5 hours the help of hundreds of participants, we covered a wall patch of 657 artistic spectre tiles!

Aperiodic monotiles have been discovered in 2023 and released for open use under a creative common attribution license: you can find all informations on the Spectre tile here and on its sister tile, the Hat tile here. An entertaining and educational introduction to what they are and the mathematics behind them can be found on Scientifc Amarican, in an article from one of the discoverers themselves: Inside Mathematicians’ Search for the Mysterious ‘Einstein Tile’.

We would like to remain on the same wavelength of the authors and release all of our material for free and open use under the same Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License.

You can download all the materials developed for our event, including all the blueprints and the turtles spectres in PDF below.
