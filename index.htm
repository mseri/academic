Research Interests

Spectral Theory, Differential Geometry, Dynamical Systems

  • Geometric integrators for on contact and sub-Riemannian flows
  • Spectral geometry on contact and sub-Riemannian manifolds
  • Spectral theory for magnetic operators and Ahronov-Bohm effect
  • Spectral theory for non-self-adjoint operators
  • Spectral analysis of singular systems
  • Ergodic theory of extended (random) dynamical systems
