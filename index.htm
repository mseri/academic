News

  • Season 3 of Degrees of Freedom, a podcast on higher education, is out. In this season I join Tassos Sarampalis as cohost. In the podcast we speak with university students and teachers about all manners of didactic methods, values, and philosophies.
  • Season 1 of aired is first episode in November 2023. Every month, Marit van Straaten and I interview mathematicians at the Bernoulli Institute to talk about mathematics and the stereotypes and biases that come with it. We hope to demistify some of them by showing the human side of mathematics and by discussing what the life of mathematician is like.
