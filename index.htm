News

  • We started the Groningen-Oldenburg-Utrecht analysis seminar. The first edition will be on February 16 2024 in Groningen. The second edition will be in Oldenburg in October 2 2024.

  • Season 4 of Degrees of Freedom, a podcast on higher education, is out with a new episode on curiosity and wonder. In the podcast we explore different theoretical, philosophical and practical aspects of education, in a dialogue with students, lecturers and other experts.

  • Season 2 of It's Not Just Numbers is coming in November, catch up with the old episodes if you haven't already done it! Every month, we interview mathematicians to talk about mathematics and the stereotypes and biases that come with it. We hope to demistify some of them by showing the human side of mathematics and by discussing what the life of mathematician is like.
